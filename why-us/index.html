The Cost of Disconnected Systems

When HMS, pharmacy, lab, and finance don't talk to each other, founders pay the price.

Silent Revenue Leakage

Procedures done in OT don't get billed because the billing desk never got notified. Pharmacy dispenses items that never post to the patient's account. Hospitals lose ₹8–25L per month this way without ever knowing.

Operational Bottlenecks

Discharges take 4 hours because nurses have to manually reconcile with pharmacy and accounts. Patient flow stalls, bed turnaround drops, and patient satisfaction plummets.

Management Blind Spots

You can't fix what you can't see. Without a unified system, founders lack real-time data on daily profitability, department inefficiencies, and inventory shrinkage.
