Why hospitals choose unified discharge billing

Zero Missing Slip Scrambles

Clinical orders—such as lab tests, nursing charges, and room updates—post directly to the financial ledger as they occur. There is no need for billing clerks to crosscheck paper logs or request manual details from ward nurses.

Reduced Discharge Wait Times

Traditional billing consolidation requires hours of manual compilation. Zip Discharge compiles inpatient billing files automatically as soon as the medical discharge is finalized by the treating doctor.

Improved Family Experience

Families spend less time at the cash counter during discharge. A smooth settlement process improves patient satisfaction, speeds up bed turnover, and enhances the hospital brand name.

How Zip Discharge Works

A single, coordinated flow of data from clinical decision to patient exit.

1

Medical Discharge Initiated

The attending physician completes the digital patient chart in the EMR and enters the discharge orders. The doctor generates and signs the digital discharge summary directly on screen.

2

Consolidated Ledger Compilation

ZenoHosp's ledger aggregates every running charge—including IPD room tariffs, diagnostics lab results, and nursing procedures. If connected to a pharmacy POS via active API contracts, pharmacy items are pulled in simultaneously.

3

TPA Splitting & Adjustments

For cashless patients, the finance engine splits TPA approvals and co-pay parameters dynamically. Cashier applies initial advance deposits and finalizes the balance invoice within seconds.

4

Digital Clearance & Receipt

The system sends the final itemized bill to the family's WhatsApp number. Once paid via UPI or card, financial clearance is updated, printing the gate pass and completing the patient exit loop.
