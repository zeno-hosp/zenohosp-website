Why hospitals leave Attune

"Analytics that tell you nothing actionable"

Attune's reporting gives you data dumps, not insights. ZenoHosp's live dashboards show bed occupancy, revenue, and doctor performance updating every minute — not at month-end.

"Custom quotes, hidden costs"

Attune pricing requires a sales call, a demo, and a proposal — and even then, module upgrades and AMCs add up. ZenoHosp publishes flat pricing: you know the number before the first call.

"ABDM compliance still catching up"

ABHA ID creation, Health Information Exchange, and DPDP Act compliance aren't native in Attune. With government ABDM deadlines tightening, this is a real operational risk.
