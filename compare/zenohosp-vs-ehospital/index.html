Why hospitals leave eHospital

Desktop-first, cloud-second

eHospital was built for local server installation. Running it on the cloud feels like an afterthought, and remote access for doctors and admins is often clunky or requires VPN setup.

Pay-per-module, forever

Every additional department — pharmacy, lab, OT — is a separate licence purchase. As hospitals grow and add departments, the costs compound unpredictably.

ABDM as an afterthought

ABHA ID creation, Health Information Exchange, and Digital Health Records are either not native or require expensive integrations. Hospitals near ABDM compliance deadlines find this painful.
