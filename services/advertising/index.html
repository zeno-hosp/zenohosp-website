    Frequently Asked Questions

    Common questions hospital founders have about our healthcare advertising services.

    What is the expected ROI for hospital advertising?
    ROI depends on the specialty and location, but our targeted Google and Facebook ad campaigns typically reduce Patient Acquisition Costs (CAC) by 30-50% while driving immediate high-intent OPD walk-ins. We focus on high-margin procedures to maximize your returns.
    How do you handle strict Google healthcare ad policies?
    Healthcare advertising policies are notoriously strict. Our certified ad specialists know exactly how to structure landing pages and ad copy to ensure 100% compliance, preventing your ad accounts from getting suspended while still delivering compelling messages.
    Do you guarantee patient footfall?
    While we cannot legally guarantee specific medical outcomes, our performance marketing strategies guarantee highly targeted impressions and clicks from patients actively searching for your specific treatments in your precise locality. We measure success by actual appointments booked.
    How much should we budget for hospital advertising?
    Most single-specialty clinics see meaningful results starting at ₹30,000–50,000/month in ad spend. Multi-specialty hospitals covering several service lines typically need ₹75,000+ to get enough data to optimize properly. We'll recommend a specific number after the free audit, based on your local competition and target procedures — not a one-size-fits-all package.
    How long until we see results?
    Google Search and Local Service Ads can start driving calls within the first week of launch, since they capture patients already searching for care. Meta and YouTube campaigns are more about building awareness and typically need 3–4 weeks of data before we can optimize them meaningfully. We report progress weekly for the first month so you're never wondering what's happening with your spend.
    Do you also create the ad creative and videos?
    Yes. Through our Social Media Management service, delivered with our partner Zesignworks, we can shoot and edit the photo and video creative your campaigns need — so you're not scrambling to find content between ad launches.
