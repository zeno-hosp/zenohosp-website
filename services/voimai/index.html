Our Hospital AI Design Architecture

Building AI for healthcare requires unparalleled precision, security, and empathy. Here is how we engineer Voim.ai specifically for hospital environments.

HIPAA Compliant & Secure

Patient data privacy is our foundation. Voim.ai operates with a strict 30-day auto-delete policy for all call recordings and transcripts. All data exchanged between the AI and your HMS is end-to-end encrypted, ensuring full regulatory compliance.

Medical Lexicon Training

Unlike generic voice bots, Voim.ai is pre-trained on complex medical terminology. Whether a patient mispronounces "Otolaryngology" or refers to an "ECG" as an "EKG", the agent accurately maps the intent to the correct hospital department.

Regional Accent & Language Engine

Hospitals in India serve diverse demographics. Voim.ai natively understands heavily accented English and seamlessly switches to regional languages like Tamil, Hindi, or Telugu mid-conversation if the patient struggles.

Instant Human Fallback

AI shouldn't handle emergencies. Our architecture includes real-time sentiment and urgency analysis. If a patient indicates severe distress or asks a complex medical question, Voim.ai instantly hot-transfers the call to a human receptionist or emergency triager.
