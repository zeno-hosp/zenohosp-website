Automated Patient Scheduling

Never Miss Another Appointment Call

See It In Action

Intelligent Inbound & Outbound Scheduling

Voim.ai seamlessly integrates with your Hospital Management System (HMS) to book, reschedule, and cancel appointments over a natural phone call.

Inbound Appointment Booking

Patients can call 24/7 to book appointments. The AI checks real-time doctor availability and schedules the visit without human intervention.

Outbound Reminders

Reduce no-shows by 40%. Voim.ai calls patients 24 hours before their appointment to confirm, reschedule, or cancel.

Waitlist Management

Automatically call patients on the waitlist when a slot opens up due to a cancellation.

Multilingual Support

Speak to patients in Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, and English, dynamically switching languages based on the caller's preference.

How Automated Scheduling Works

A seamless, end-to-end flow from the first ring to a confirmed booking in your HMS.

1

Patient Calls the Hospital

The patient calls the hospital's main line. Voim.ai picks up immediately, skipping hold music, and asks how it can help.

2

Intent Recognition & Doctor Lookup

The AI understands the request ("I need to see Dr. Sharma tomorrow"), checks Dr. Sharma's real-time availability in the HMS, and offers available slots.

3

Booking & Confirmation

Once the patient agrees, the AI books the slot in the HMS. It immediately triggers an automated WhatsApp and SMS confirmation to the patient's phone.
