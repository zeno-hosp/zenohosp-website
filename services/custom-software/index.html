    Frequently Asked Questions

    Common questions hospital founders ask before commissioning custom software.

    Can you integrate custom modules with our existing Hospital Management System?

    Yes — we build bespoke modules via APIs (HL7, FHIR) that integrate directly with your existing HMS, lab equipment, and third-party billing portals, whether or not you're already on ZenoHosp.

    How much does custom software actually cost?

    Because we extend an existing platform instead of building from a blank file, most single-module projects cost significantly less than hiring a generalist dev agency to build the same thing from scratch. You get a fixed quote after the free process-mapping session — no hourly retainer.

    Will the software really match how we already work, or will we still have to adapt?

    Our analysts document your actual workflow first — including the workarounds and exceptions that off-the-shelf software usually ignores — and build to that. If a step in your process is genuinely inefficient, we'll tell you, but we don't force a redesign you didn't ask for.

    Is the custom healthcare software secure and audit-ready?

    Yes. Every custom module ships with data encryption, role-based access control, and full audit trails, built to the same standard as our core HMS — see our full security overview.

    Why choose custom hospital software over off-the-shelf solutions?

    Generic software forces your hospital to adapt its workflows to the system. Custom software adapts to your unique processes, saving doctors time, reducing errors, and improving operational efficiency.
