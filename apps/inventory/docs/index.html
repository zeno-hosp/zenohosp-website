Inventory Dashboard

The central hub for tracking hospital supply chains, pharmaceuticals, and critical stock levels.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

At a glance, the dashboard provides a high-level summary of your inventory health:

  • Total Products: The number of distinct active items registered in the Item Master (e.g., Paracetamol, Syringes, Patient Monitors).
  • Low Stock Items: Immediately identifies items that have fallen below their configured Minimum Reorder Level and urgently require a Purchase Order.
  • Total Transactions: The volume of stock movement (GRN receipts, departmental consumption, stock adjustments) recorded in the last 24 hours.
  • System Status: Displays the operational status of the inventory server and database sync.

Critical Stock Alerts

This section is crucial for preventing stock-outs of life-saving equipment and medicines. It lists the specific product names, their current stock across the hospital, their configured Minimum Level, and provides a quick-action button (+ Log Stock) to manually adjust the inventory if a physical audit discovers unrecorded stock.

Recent Transactions

A live ledger showing every inventory action as it happens in real-time. It displays:

  • Type: The nature of the movement (e.g., ACTIVITY, RECEIPT, CONSUMPTION).
  • Product & Qty: The specific item and whether stock was added (+ positive) or removed (- negative).
  • User & Timestamp: A strict audit trail showing exactly which logged-in user executed the transaction and at what precise moment.
