Department Indenting

Departments request stock. Store manager reviews. Stock issued.

Each department submits an indent request in ZenoHosp specifying the items and quantities needed. The store manager reviews the request against available stock and consumption history. Approved indents are issued and the issuance is recorded against the requesting department.

  • Departments submit indent requests from their own screen
  • Store manager reviews indent against stock and consumption history
  • Approved indents issued — recorded against the department
  • Rejected indents documented with reason

Consumption Analytics

Which departments are consuming what — and whether it's normal.

Monthly consumption per department is visible in ZenoHosp — both in quantity and rupee value. ZenoHosp tracks average monthly consumption per department and flags deviations. If the ICU suddenly uses 40% more IV sets than usual, the system flags it for investigation.

  • Monthly consumption per department in quantity and value
  • Average consumption baseline per department per item
  • Deviation alerts when consumption exceeds baseline significantly
  • Top-consuming items per department ranked for review

Cost Allocation

Finance gets accurate department-wise consumable costs.

Every issuance in ZenoHosp is valued at the purchase price (or weighted average). The Finance module receives department-wise consumable cost reports — actual cost of consumables used by each department per period. No estimation. No approximation.

  • Consumable costs allocated to departments at purchase price
  • Weighted average cost option for accurate valuation
  • Department P&L in Finance reflects actual consumable costs
  • Monthly cost allocation report available for Finance sign-off

Frequently asked questions

How does ZenoHosp track consumption per department?

Every issuance from the store is recorded against the requesting department. Whether it's a direct issuance or against an indent, the item, quantity, value, and department are all logged.

Can department heads see their own consumption?

Yes. Each department head has access to their department's consumption dashboard — showing items consumed, quantities, and cost for any period they select.

How does ZenoHosp alert for over-consumption?

ZenoHosp tracks average monthly consumption per department per item. When consumption in a given month significantly exceeds the average, an alert is generated for the store manager and department head.
