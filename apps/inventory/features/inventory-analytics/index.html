Stock Turnover Analytics

Which items are moving fast. Which are sitting on the shelf.

Stock turnover rate ranks every item from fastest to slowest moving. Fast movers need frequent reorders - their min levels should be set higher. Slow movers with high stock on hand and approaching expiry need attention. ZenoHosp surfaces these automatically.

  • Turnover rate per item: fast, medium, slow categories
  • Over-stocked slow movers flagged for procurement adjustment
  • Under-stocked fast movers flagged for min-level review
  • Consumption forecast: expected days of stock remaining per item

Vendor Performance

Which suppliers deliver on time. And which don't.

Every purchase order delivery is tracked against the promised lead time. ZenoHosp calculates the on-time delivery rate and average lead time per supplier. Suppliers with poor performance are identified for review. This data drives supplier selection for future orders.

  • On-time delivery rate per supplier over selected period
  • Average actual lead time vs. quoted lead time comparison
  • Fill rate: percentage of ordered quantity delivered as requested
  • Supplier performance score for annual vendor review

Wastage & Cost Analysis

Every rupee lost to expiry and damage - tracked and explained.

Wastage from expiry and damage is valued at purchase price and reported monthly. ZenoHosp tracks whether wastage is improving or worsening month on month. The items driving the most wastage are ranked. Management can set wastage reduction targets and track progress.

  • Wastage by reason: expiry, damage, theft, disposal - categorised
  • Monthly wastage value in rupees per item and per location
  • Wastage trend: improving or worsening month-on-month
  • Top 10 wastage items ranked by rupee value

Frequently asked questions

What inventory analytics does ZenoHosp provide?

ZenoHosp provides stock turnover analysis, live inventory value, wastage trend tracking by reason code, vendor performance reports, consumption forecasting, and department-wise cost allocation.

Can we see total inventory value in real time?

Yes. The inventory dashboard shows total stock value across all locations, updated in real time as items are received, issued, and disposed.

How does ZenoHosp track vendor performance?

Every purchase order delivery is tracked against the promised lead time. ZenoHosp calculates on-time delivery rate, average lead time, and fill rate per supplier over any selected period.
