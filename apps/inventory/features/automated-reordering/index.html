Reorder Configuration

Set it once. Never think about stockouts again.

Each inventory item has a minimum stock level configured per location. When stock falls to the minimum, ZenoHosp generates an alert and optionally creates a purchase order to the preferred supplier for the configured standard order quantity. The store manager gets a WhatsApp notification.

  • Min/max stock levels per item per location - configured once
  • Preferred supplier linked to each item for auto-PO generation
  • Standard order quantity per item - reduces manual decision-making
  • WhatsApp notification to store manager when PO is auto-generated

Purchase Order Workflow

PO generated. Manager reviews. Supplier receives.

Auto-generated purchase orders go into a pending approval queue. The store manager reviews the items, quantities, and supplier - adjusts if needed - and approves. The approved PO is sent to the supplier. ZenoHosp tracks delivery against the PO.

  • Auto-generated POs visible in approval queue immediately
  • Store manager reviews and approves before dispatch to supplier
  • PO can be edited before approval - adjust quantities or supplier
  • Approved PO sent to supplier via email or WhatsApp

GRN & Receipt

Goods received. Quantities verified. Stock updated.

When goods arrive, the receiving clerk scans or enters the received quantities against the purchase order. Discrepancies are flagged - received less than ordered, damaged goods, or wrong items. Accepted quantities are added to stock immediately.

  • Receive against purchase order - discrepancies flagged automatically
  • Barcode scanning for fast GRN entry
  • Partial deliveries handled - remaining balance tracked
  • Batch number and expiry date captured at receipt

Frequently asked questions

How does automatic reordering work in ZenoHosp?

Each item has a configured minimum stock level. When stock falls to the minimum, ZenoHosp generates a reorder alert and optionally creates a purchase order to the preferred supplier for the standard order quantity. The store manager approves before the PO is sent.

Can the store manager review and modify the auto-generated PO?

Yes. Auto-generated POs go into a pending approval queue. The store manager can adjust quantities, change the supplier, or cancel before approving and sending to the supplier.

How are received goods matched to purchase orders?

When goods arrive, the store staff receive against the purchase order in ZenoHosp. Discrepancies - short deliveries, wrong items - are flagged. Accepted quantities update stock immediately with batch number and expiry date captured.
