    Frequently asked questions

    How does ZenoHosp track stock across multiple store locations?

    All locations - main store, OT store, pharmacy, ward pantries - are tracked in one system. The inventory dashboard shows stock per location and total across all locations simultaneously.

    Does ZenoHosp support barcode scanning?

    Yes. Standard USB barcode scanners are supported. Scan items on receipt, issue, and stock count. Every scan updates stock levels in real time.

    How does the low stock alert work?

    Each item has a minimum stock level configured per location. When stock falls below the minimum, an alert is generated. If automated reordering is configured, a purchase order can be triggered automatically.
