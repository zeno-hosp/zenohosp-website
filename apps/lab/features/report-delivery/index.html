Frequently asked questions

When does the patient receive their WhatsApp report?

The moment a pathologist validates and signs off the results, ZenoHosp generates the PDF and sends it to the patient's WhatsApp number. This typically happens within 2 minutes of sign-off.

Does the ordering doctor see the result automatically?

Yes. When a report is released, the result is simultaneously posted to the patient's EMR in HMS. The ordering doctor sees it in their patient dashboard without any manual action.

Can reports be shared with other hospitals?

Yes. For ABHA-enrolled patients, reports can be shared via the ABHA health record ecosystem with patient consent - accessible to any ABDM-participating hospital.
