Frequently asked questions about hospital LIS software in India

Does ZenoHosp Lab integrate with the HMS module?

Yes. Lab is built into the ZenoHosp platform. When a doctor orders a test in the EMR, it appears instantly on the phlebotomy worklist. Completed reports are pushed back to the patient's record automatically — no re-entry required.

Which lab analysers does it support?

ZenoHosp Lab supports HL7 and ASTM protocols, which cover most major analysers — including Sysmex, Roche Cobas, Mindray, Abbott Architect, and BioMérieux. Both unidirectional and bidirectional interfaces are supported. Contact us for a specific compatibility check.

How does the pathologist sign-off workflow work?

After a lab technician enters results, they are queued for pathologist review. The pathologist sees a colour-coded view of abnormal values, can compare with historical results, and approves with a digital signature. The report is only released for delivery after sign-off.

How are reports delivered to patients?

Once validated, ZenoHosp Lab automatically generates a branded PDF and delivers it via the patient's preferred channel — SMS link, WhatsApp, or email. Reports are also attached directly to the patient's EMR record in HMS.

Can the lab module handle microbiology and culture reports?

Yes. ZenoHosp Lab supports culture and sensitivity reports with multi-day TAT tracking. You can configure organism templates, antibiotic sensitivity panels, and multi-stage result entry for micro reports that go through different processing stages.

Is there support for outsourced / external lab tests?

Yes. Tests that are outsourced to external reference labs can be tracked within the same worklist. You can record the external lab name, expected TAT, and upload scanned reports — keeping the patient's full diagnostic history in one place.
