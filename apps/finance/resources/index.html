Implementation Guides & FAQs

How long does Finance implementation take?

ZenoHosp Finance is typically deployed as part of the overall ZenoHosp go-live - within the 90-day guaranteed timeline. For standalone Finance deployments, most hospitals are live within 30 days.

Does Finance integrate with TPA portals?

Yes. You can generate claim XMLs and track the status of submissions directly from the Finance module. Remittances are automatically reconciled against patient accounts.

How do we migrate historical accounting data?

Our implementation team provides a standard CSV template for Opening Balances, Vendor Ledgers, and outstanding patient dues. This is imported on Go-Live day.
