Incentive Structure Configuration

Configure once. Applied every month automatically.

Each doctor's incentive structure is configured in ZenoHosp - flat fee per consultation, percentage of OPD or IPD collections, tiered slab (higher percentage above a threshold), or a combination. The configuration is applied automatically to every revenue event attributed to that doctor.

  • Flat fee: fixed amount per consultation or procedure
  • Percentage: defined % of collections generated
  • Tiered slab: higher % above revenue threshold
  • Combination: flat fee plus percentage for procedures

Monthly Calculation

Every collection attributed. Every payout calculated. No disputes.

At the end of each month, ZenoHosp calculates each doctor's incentive from their actual revenue data. The calculation is transparent - each doctor can see the collections attributed to them and the incentive formula applied. No surprises. No spreadsheet errors.

  • Revenue per doctor compiled from all billing events
  • Incentive formula applied to actual collections
  • Deductions for advances or loans applied automatically
  • Final payout amount calculated before payroll approval

Statement & Payroll

Incentive statement sent to doctor. Payout included in payroll.

Each doctor receives an incentive statement via WhatsApp - showing their collections, the formula applied, and the payout amount. The amount is passed to the People module for inclusion in the monthly payroll run. Finance approves. HR runs payroll.

  • Incentive statement sent to doctor via WhatsApp or email

Frequently asked questions

What incentive structures can be configured in ZenoHosp?

ZenoHosp supports flat fee per consultation, percentage of collections, tiered slabs (higher percentage above thresholds), and combinations. Each doctor can have a different structure.

How does ZenoHosp calculate monthly incentives?

At month-end, ZenoHosp compiles all billing events attributed to each doctor, applies the configured incentive formula, and produces the monthly payout amount. The calculation is visible to both the doctor and finance.

Is the incentive payout included in payroll?

Yes. After Finance approves the incentive statements, the payout amounts are passed to the People module for inclusion in the monthly payroll run.
