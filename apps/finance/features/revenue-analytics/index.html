Frequently asked questions

How is department revenue calculated in ZenoHosp?

Revenue is attributed to the department that generated each billing event. OPD consultation revenue goes to the consulting department, procedure charges go to the performing department. Pharmacy and lab revenue are tracked separately.

Can doctor revenue be used for incentive calculation?

Yes. Doctor revenue data from the Finance module feeds directly into the People module's incentive calculation. Monthly payouts are calculated automatically from actual revenue - no manual spreadsheets.

How real-time is the revenue dashboard?

Revenue data is updated as billing events happen. There is no overnight refresh. The dashboard shows revenue up to the last completed transaction.
