Asset Dashboard

The control center for monitoring hospital infrastructure, equipment health, and warranties.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Your dashboard immediately surfaces the most critical operational metrics regarding physical assets:

  • Total Inventory: The sheer volume of registered physical assets (from stethoscopes and beds to MRI machines).
  • Medical Equipment: A specific count of clinically relevant assets, separating them from IT hardware or general furniture.
  • Warranty Active: Highlights how many machines are currently protected under their manufacturer's warranty or an active AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract).
  • In Maintenance: A critical metric showing exactly how many assets are currently out of order or undergoing scheduled repairs, which impacts clinical capacity.

Quick Actions

From the dashboard, you can quickly jump into the most common workflows, such as clicking + Add Asset to onboard newly purchased equipment.
