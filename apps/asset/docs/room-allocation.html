Room Allocation

Map your physical assets directly to rooms across the hospital.

Integration with HMS

The Asset module pulls physical location data directly from the HMS Infrastructure Master. You do not need to re-create Wards, Floors, or OT Rooms here.

Allocating Equipment

Knowing what you own is only half the battle; knowing where it is located is equally important.

  1. Navigate to Room Allocation and select a location (e.g., "ICU Bed 4").
  2. Click Map Asset.
  3. Search for the specific Asset Code (e.g., MON-003).

By mapping assets to specific rooms, nurses and technicians can instantly locate vital equipment during emergencies, and auditors can perform room-by-room physical checks efficiently.
