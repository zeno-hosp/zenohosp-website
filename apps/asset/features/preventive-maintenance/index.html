Breakdown & Downtime

Every breakdown recorded. Resolved faster.

When equipment goes down, the breakdown is logged with the time, reported by, and failure description. The system assigns it to the responsible technician or vendor. Resolution time is tracked. Downtime reports help identify chronically unreliable equipment.

  • Breakdown logged with timestamp and failure description
  • Assignment to internal technician or external vendor
  • Resolution time tracked from breakdown to clearance
  • Downtime report per equipment - for replacement planning

Frequently asked questions

How are maintenance schedules created in ZenoHosp?

You configure the service interval for each equipment - daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, or annual. ZenoHosp generates maintenance tasks automatically based on that schedule and assigns them to the responsible technician.

How does ZenoHosp track AMC expiry?

Every AMC is registered with vendor details, coverage scope, and expiry date. ZenoHosp sends renewal alerts 30 days and 7 days before the contract expires.

Is the maintenance documentation NABH-compliant?

Yes. Maintenance records, calibration certificates, and breakdown logs are documented in formats aligned with NABH accreditation requirements.
