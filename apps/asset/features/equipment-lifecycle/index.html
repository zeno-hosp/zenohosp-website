Purchase & Commissioning

Every equipment's life starts with a complete record.

When equipment is purchased, ZenoHosp records the invoice, vendor, purchase price, warranty details, and commissioning date. The depreciation method and rate are configured. The equipment goes live in the asset register — ready for maintenance scheduling, location tracking, and utilisation recording.

  • Purchase invoice, vendor, and price recorded at registration
  • Warranty period and expiry date tracked
  • Depreciation method configured: straight-line or reducing balance
  • Commissioning date set — depreciation calculation starts from here

Depreciation Tracking

Book value calculated automatically every month.

ZenoHosp calculates depreciation for every asset using the configured method and rate — straight-line or reducing balance. Book value is updated monthly. At any point, management can see the current book value and accumulated depreciation of any equipment or the entire asset portfolio.

  • Straight-line depreciation: equal amount per year
  • Reducing balance: percentage of current book value per year
  • Book value updated monthly without manual calculation
  • Asset-wise and portfolio depreciation schedule available

Frequently asked questions

What depreciation methods does ZenoHosp support?

ZenoHosp supports straight-line depreciation (equal annual amount) and reducing balance depreciation (percentage of current book value per year). The method is configured per asset or asset category.

How does ZenoHosp help with replacement planning?

The lifecycle dashboard shows each equipment's residual value, cumulative maintenance cost, utilisation rate, and downtime history. Management can compare the cost of continued maintenance against the replacement cost to make data-backed decisions.

Is depreciation calculated automatically?

Yes. ZenoHosp calculates depreciation and updates book value monthly for every registered asset using the configured method and rate. No manual calculation required.
