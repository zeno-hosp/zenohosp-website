Stock Analytics

Know which drugs to order more of — and which to stop over-ordering.

Stock turnover rate shows which drugs are dispensed quickly (fast movers needing frequent reorder) and which are sitting on the shelf (slow movers with high expiry risk). ZenoHosp ranks all drugs by turnover rate and flags anomalies.

  • Stock turnover rate per drug: fast, medium, slow mover categories
  • Slow movers with high expiry risk flagged for action
  • Over-stocked items identified — reduce future order quantity
  • Under-stocked items identified — increase min-max levels

Revenue Analytics

Pharmacy revenue by day, week, month — and by drug category.

Daily collections, month-to-date revenue, and year-on-year comparison — all in the pharmacy dashboard. Revenue is broken down by prescription vs. OTC, by drug category, and by payer type (cash, TPA, CGHS). No manual compilation.

  • Daily and MTD pharmacy revenue from the dashboard
  • Revenue by category: prescription vs OTC, by drug type
  • Payer breakdown: cash, UPI, TPA, CGHS — proportions visible
  • Year-on-year comparison for the same period

Dispensing Pattern Analysis

Which doctors prescribe what. Which departments use what.

Dispensing pattern reports show which doctors prescribe which drugs most frequently, which departments drive the highest pharmacy volumes, and which drugs are prescribed outside their standard indication. Useful for formulary management and cost control.

  • Prescribing pattern by doctor — top 10 prescribed drugs per doctor
  • Department-wise pharmacy consumption for cost allocation
  • High-value drug dispensing report for finance review
  • Drug substitution analysis — generic vs. brand dispensing rate

Frequently asked questions

What analytics does ZenoHosp provide for pharmacy stock management?

ZenoHosp provides stock turnover analysis showing fast and slow movers, over-stocked and under-stocked identification, expiry wastage trend tracking, and purchase vs. consumption comparison.

Can we track pharmacy revenue separately from HMS billing?

Yes. Pharmacy revenue is visible as a separate line in ZenoHosp — by day, week, month, and category. It also rolls up into the HMS Finance module for consolidated hospital P&L.

Are dispensing pattern reports available per doctor?

Yes. The prescribing pattern report shows each doctor's top 10 prescribed drugs, helping pharmacy managers anticipate demand and identify opportunities for generic substitution.
