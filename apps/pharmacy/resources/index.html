Common questions about Pharmacy

How long does Pharmacy implementation take?

ZenoHosp Pharmacy is typically deployed as part of the overall ZenoHosp go-live — within the 90-day guaranteed timeline. For standalone Pharmacy deployments, most hospitals are live within 30 days.

Does Pharmacy integrate with HMS and other modules?

Yes. All ZenoHosp modules share a single patient database. Pharmacy reads from and writes to the same record — no separate integrations required.

Is training included?

Yes. Our implementation team trains all relevant staff roles on Pharmacy as part of go-live. Role-specific training ensures each team member learns only what applies to their workflow.
