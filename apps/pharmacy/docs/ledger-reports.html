Ledger & Reports

Generate analytical exports for auditing, compliance, and end-of-day financial reconciliation.

Available Reports

1. Daily Sales Report (DSR)

A mandatory report run at the end of every pharmacist's shift. It details total cash collected, UPI transactions, and card swipes. The cashier must hand over this exact cash amount to the Finance team for deposit.

2. Stock Valuation Report

Calculates the total monetary value of all medicines currently sitting on the pharmacy shelves based on their Purchase Rate. Crucial for end-of-financial-year accounting.

3. Fast-Moving vs Slow-Moving Items

Analyzes sales velocity. Helps management decide which drugs to buy in bulk for better margins, and which drugs to stop ordering to prevent dead stock accumulation.

4. GST Output Register

A clean, CA-ready export of all GST collected from patients during sales, formatted for easy filing on the Government GST portal.
