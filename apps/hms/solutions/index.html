Core workflows HMS solves

OPD workflow - from token to prescription Patient gets a token, waits in queue, sees the doctor, gets a prescription, collects medicine from pharmacy. Every step is recorded, timed, and connected. The doctor never asks "did the patient register?" - the queue tells them.
IPD admission to discharge Patient admitted from OPD or emergency, assigned a bed, daily ward rounds recorded, procedures logged, diet and nursing notes added. At discharge, billing has every charge automatically - no manual entry from ward staff.
Emergency department triage Patients arriving in emergency are triaged and registered in parallel. Vitals, chief complaint, and urgency level are captured immediately. The attending doctor sees the triage note before they reach the patient.
Cross-department patient movement Patient goes from OPD to radiology to lab to billing - the same record follows them. Each department adds to it. No re-entry at any point. The billing desk sees every charge as it's incurred.
Insurance and TPA cashless admission When a TPA patient is admitted, the pre-authorisation request goes out from the admission desk. Approved amount is recorded. At discharge, the bill is already split - TPA portion and patient co-pay - ready for settlement.
