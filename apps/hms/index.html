Frequently Asked Questions about Hospital Management Software in India

What is the best hospital management software in India?

ZenoHosp is one of the best hospital management software solutions in India, offering comprehensive features including patient registration, EMR, billing, appointment scheduling, and seamless integration with OT, pharmacy, and inventory modules. It's trusted by hospitals across South India.

How much does HMS software cost in India?

ZenoHosp offers flexible pricing plans suitable for small clinics to large hospitals. Contact us for a free demo and customized pricing based on your facility's needs. We offer cloud-based solutions that reduce IT infrastructure costs.

Is ZenoHosp HMS cloud-based?

Yes, ZenoHosp is a cloud-based hospital management system that can be accessed from anywhere. This ensures data security, automatic backups, and eliminates the need for expensive on-premise servers.

Does the HMS support EMR and digital health records?

Yes, ZenoHosp includes a comprehensive Electronic Medical Records (EMR) system that allows doctors to digitally record patient history, prescriptions, lab reports, and treatment plans in compliance with Indian healthcare regulations.

Can ZenoHosp integrate with existing hospital systems?

Yes, ZenoHosp is designed to integrate with existing laboratory systems, diagnostic equipment, and third-party software through APIs. Our team provides implementation support for hospitals in India.

Is the billing software GST compliant?

Yes, ZenoHosp billing module is fully GST compliant and supports all Indian tax requirements. It generates GST-ready invoices and maintains proper documentation for audits.
