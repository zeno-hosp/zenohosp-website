Frequently asked questions

How real-time is the dashboard data?

Live data — OPD queue, bed occupancy, today's revenue — refreshes every 60 seconds. Historical reports are generated on-demand. There is no overnight batch process.

Can different roles see different dashboards?

Yes. The Medical Director sees hospital-wide metrics. Department heads see their department's volume and revenue. Billing staff see outstanding amounts and TPA claim status. Each role sees only what's within their access permissions.

What MIS reports does ZenoHosp HMS generate?

30+ standard reports including daily collection summary, OPD attendance by doctor, IPD census, department-wise revenue, doctor-wise revenue and incentive calculation, bed occupancy, and TPA claim status. Custom reports can be built using the report builder.

Can we schedule reports to be sent automatically?

Yes. Any report can be configured to run on a schedule — daily, weekly, or monthly — and emailed to specified recipients. No manual intervention required.

Can we build custom reports?

Yes. The ZenoHosp report builder lets you select dimensions and metrics. Saved templates can be accessed by authorised staff at any time.

How does ZenoHosp track doctor performance?

The doctor performance report shows patients seen, revenue generated, appointment completion rate, and referral activity. Used for incentive calculations in the Finance module. Doctors see their own stats; only management sees all doctors.
