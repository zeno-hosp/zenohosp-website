Frequently asked questions

What billing items are synced automatically?

ZenoHosp pulls room rates, procedure costs (OT, ICU), diagnostics laboratory work, imaging, nursing care charges, and consumables. Standalone pharmacy systems are synced through custom API integrations.

Does Zip Discharge require special training?

No. ZenoHosp's interface is designed for regular ward nurses and cash desk clerks, mimicking standard paper-based clearance checklists digitally.
