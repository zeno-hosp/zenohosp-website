Frequently asked questions

How fast is patient registration in ZenoHosp HMS?

New patients are registered in under 2 minutes. The average across our 75+ hospital customer base is 1 minute 48 seconds. Returning patients take under 40 seconds.

What is ABHA health ID and does ZenoHosp support it?

ABHA is a 14-digit health ID under ABDM. ZenoHosp is fully ABDM-compliant - link or create ABHA IDs directly from the registration screen.

Can we import existing patient records?

Yes. Our team migrates your complete patient database as part of go-live. Included in the standard package at no additional cost.

Does the system prevent duplicate patient registrations?

Yes. Before creating a new record, ZenoHosp checks phone number, Aadhaar, and ABHA ID. Potential duplicates are flagged for review.

Does ZenoHosp work offline?

Yes. Critical registration functions queue locally when internet is unavailable and sync to the cloud when connectivity returns.

Is patient data HIPAA-compliant?

Yes. AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit, role-based access control, and a permanent audit trail on every record.
