Frequently asked questions

How does ZenoHosp handle walk-in patients alongside pre-booked appointments?

Both run simultaneously. Walk-in patients receive a token number at the front desk. The doctor's screen shows a combined view - pre-booked appointments by time slot and walk-in tokens in sequence. The front desk manages both from one screen without switching systems.

How do WhatsApp appointment reminders work?

When an appointment is booked, ZenoHosp schedules automated WhatsApp messages - one 24 hours before, one 2 hours before. The message includes the doctor's name, time, and a link to reschedule or cancel. No manual follow-up required. Hospitals using this feature see a 14% average reduction in no-shows.

Can patients book appointments online themselves?

Yes. You can optionally provide patients a booking link. They select the doctor, available date, and time slot. The booking appears in the appointment calendar immediately.

What happens when a patient cancels?

The slot opens automatically and any waitlisted patients are notified via WhatsApp. Cancellation history is recorded against the patient's profile.

Can we set different appointment durations per doctor?

Yes. A general physician might have 10-minute slots while a specialist takes 20 minutes. Slots can also vary by appointment type. The calendar enforces these rules automatically.

How are public holidays and doctor leaves managed?

Doctor leaves, conference blocks, and public holidays are configured in advance in ZenoHosp. The booking system automatically blocks those periods - patients cannot book on leave days and existing appointments trigger a reschedule prompt.
