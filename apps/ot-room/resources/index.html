Common questions about OT Room

How long does OT Room implementation take?

ZenoHosp OT Room is typically deployed as part of the overall ZenoHosp go-live — within the 90-day guaranteed timeline. For standalone OT Room deployments, most hospitals are live within 30 days.

Does OT Room integrate with HMS and other modules?

Yes. All ZenoHosp modules share a single patient database. OT Room reads from and writes to the same record — no separate integrations required.

Is training included?

Yes. Our implementation team trains all relevant staff roles on OT Room as part of go-live. Role-specific training ensures each team member learns only what applies to their workflow.
