Frequently asked questions

How does ZenoHosp check surgeon availability before assignment?

ZenoHosp reads the duty roster directly from the People module. When you attempt to assign a surgeon, the system checks their schedule for that date and time. If they're on leave, in another OT, or off duty, the conflict is flagged before the assignment is saved.

Does the surgical team receive automatic notifications?

Yes. When a surgical team assignment is confirmed, every team member receives a WhatsApp message with the case details — patient, procedure, OT number, time, and team. Schedule changes trigger a new notification.

Can we manage on-call anaesthetist assignments?

Yes. The on-call schedule for anaesthetists is configured in the People module. ZenoHosp OT Room reads this schedule during team assignment, showing which anaesthetist is on call for each time slot.
