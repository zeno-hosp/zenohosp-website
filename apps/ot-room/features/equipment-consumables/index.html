Equipment Availability

Book a surgery - equipment availability confirmed at the same moment.

When scheduling a procedure, ZenoHosp checks whether the required equipment - laparoscopy tower, C-arm, cardiac bypass machine - is available and not under maintenance. Equipment booked for another case is shown as unavailable. Maintenance windows are blocked in the calendar.

  • Equipment linked to procedure type - required items auto-checked
  • Maintenance windows blocked - equipment unavailable during servicing
  • Cross-OT equipment conflicts caught before booking is confirmed
  • Equipment reservation confirmed at booking - no last-minute discovers

Consumable Usage Tracking

Every item used in surgery - tracked and deducted from inventory.

Sutures, surgical pads, IV cannulas, gloves, drapes - consumables used per case are recorded in ZenoHosp. Quantities are deducted from the OT store inventory in real time. Month-end stock counts match because every usage was recorded during the case.

  • Consumables linked to procedure type - expected items pre-filled
  • Actual quantities used recorded during or after the case
  • Deducted from OT store inventory automatically
  • Variance report - expected vs. actual usage per case type

Instrument Checklist

Every instrument accounted for. Before the patient enters. After they leave.

Instrument sets are configured in ZenoHosp - each tray has a defined item list. Before surgery, the scrub nurse checks items off against the configured set. After surgery, the count is verified again. Missing instruments are flagged before the patient leaves the OT.

  • Instrument set lists configured per procedure type
  • Pre-surgery count - verify all instruments present before patient entry
  • Post-surgery count - verify all instruments retrieved
  • Missing items trigger an immediate alert

Frequently asked questions

How does ZenoHosp prevent equipment conflicts across multiple OTs?

When scheduling a procedure that requires specific equipment, ZenoHosp checks if that equipment is already booked in another OT for the same time. Conflicts are flagged before the booking is confirmed.

Are consumables automatically deducted from OT store inventory?

Yes. When consumable usage is recorded for a case, the quantities are deducted from the OT store in the Inventory module automatically. No separate stock entry is needed.

How does the instrument checklist work?

Instrument sets are configured in ZenoHosp with a defined item list. Before the patient enters the OT, the scrub nurse verifies each item against the list. After surgery, the count is verified again. Missing instruments trigger an immediate alert.
