Frequently asked questions

How does ZenoHosp prevent OT double-bookings?

The system checks OT availability, surgeon schedule, anaesthetist roster, and equipment status simultaneously before confirming a booking. Conflicts are blocked before they're confirmed.

Can emergency surgeries be added to a full OT schedule?

Yes. Emergency cases can be inserted into the schedule with an urgency flag. The system adjusts surrounding bookings and notifies the team automatically.

How does ZenoHosp track OT cancellations?

Every cancelled surgery is recorded with the reason - patient condition, equipment failure, surgeon unavailability, or other. Cancellation reports are available by surgeon, department, and reason code.
