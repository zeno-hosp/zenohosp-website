Cancellation Analysis

Every cancelled surgery explained. Root causes identified.

When a surgery is cancelled, the reason is recorded — patient condition, equipment failure, surgeon unavailability, anaesthesia concern, or other. ZenoHosp aggregates these across all cancellations to show which surgeons, departments, and reason codes are driving cancellation rates.

  • Cancellation rate by surgeon, department, and OT
  • Reason code analysis — top causes identified automatically
  • Late cancellation vs. advance cancellation tracked separately
  • Trend analysis — is the cancellation rate improving or worsening

Frequently asked questions

What OT utilisation metrics does ZenoHosp track?

ZenoHosp tracks scheduled time vs. actual surgical time per OT, utilisation rate by theatre, department, and surgeon, unutilised slot identification, and benchmark vs. actual comparison.

Can we see why surgeries are being cancelled?

Yes. Every cancellation requires a reason code — patient condition, equipment failure, surgeon unavailability, anaesthesia concern. ZenoHosp aggregates these across all cancellations to show the top drivers of cancellation rates.

How is turnaround time calculated?

Turnaround time is the time from the previous patient leaving the OT to the next patient entering. ZenoHosp tracks this automatically from OT status timestamps — no manual recording required.
