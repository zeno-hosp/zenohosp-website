Frequently Asked Questions about OT Management Software in India

What is Operation Theatre Management Software?

OT Management Software is a digital solution that helps hospitals schedule surgeries, coordinate surgical teams, track OT equipment, and manage pre-operative and post-operative care. ZenoHosp OT module automates these processes for hospitals in India.

How does ZenoHosp OT scheduling work?

ZenoHosp OT scheduling allows hospitals to book operation theatres, assign surgeons and staff, avoid scheduling conflicts, and optimize OT utilization. The system provides real-time visibility of all OT slots.

Can the OT software track surgical equipment?

Yes, ZenoHosp OT module includes equipment tracking that monitors the availability and sterilization status of surgical instruments, ensuring everything is ready for procedures.

Does it integrate with the Hospital Management System?

Yes, the OT Management module seamlessly integrates with ZenoHosp HMS, pharmacy, and inventory systems, ensuring patient data flows automatically between departments.
