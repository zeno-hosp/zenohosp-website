Monthly Payroll Run

Attendance in. Leaves applied. Payroll calculated. Done.

At the end of every month, ZenoHosp pulls attendance data from the biometric system, applies approved leaves and LOP deductions, and calculates gross pay for every employee. PF at 12% (employee) + 13% (employer), ESI at 0.75%, and PT at state-specific slab - all calculated without a spreadsheet.

  • Attendance pulled from biometric devices automatically
  • Approved leaves applied, LOP deductions calculated
  • Gross pay calculation with all allowances and night shift additions
  • PF, ESI, PT, TDS deductions calculated per Indian statutory rates

Frequently asked questions

Does ZenoHosp automatically calculate PF and ESI?

Yes. Once salary structures are configured, ZenoHosp calculates PF (12% employee, 13% employer), ESI (0.75% employee, 3.25% employer), and PT automatically every month - applying wage ceilings and eligibility rules correctly.

Are EPFO and ESIC challans generated?

Yes. EPFO challans in ECR format and ESIC challans are generated automatically and ready for portal submission. No manual compilation.

Is Form 16 generated automatically?

Yes. At the end of the financial year, ZenoHosp generates Form 16 Part A (TDS certificate) and Part B for every employee. Ready for digital distribution to staff.

Does ZenoHosp support salary structures with multiple components?

Yes. Each employee or grade can have a configured salary structure - basic, HRA, conveyance, medical, night shift allowances, and custom components. Deductions like PF, ESI, PT, and loans are applied automatically.
