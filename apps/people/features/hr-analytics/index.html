Headcount Analytics

Know exactly how many people are in every department - right now.

The headcount dashboard shows current staff count by department, role, and shift type. New joiners and exits update the count in real time. Departments below minimum staffing levels are flagged. The CMO can see nursing coverage across all wards without making phone calls.

  • Live headcount by department, role, and employment type
  • Minimum staffing alerts when department falls below threshold
  • Joining and exit trends - net headcount change per month
  • Nurse-to-patient ratio by ward for clinical governance

Attendance & Absenteeism

Which departments have low attendance - and when it happens.

Attendance rate reports show the percentage of staff present vs. rostered per department, per shift, and per date range. Departments with chronic absenteeism are identified. Peak absenteeism periods - weekends, post-holidays - are visible for roster planning.

  • Attendance rate by department: % present vs. rostered
  • Chronic absenteeism: employees with repeated unplanned absence
  • Peak absenteeism dates: weekends, holidays, post-holidays
  • Late arrival analysis: which departments have the most late arrivals

Attrition & Payroll Cost

Exit trends and payroll cost - linked for workforce planning.

Attrition analysis shows exits by reason (resignation, retirement, termination), by department, and by role. Payroll cost by department is tracked monthly - total cost per department, cost per employee, and year-on-year trend. This data links workforce planning to financial planning.

  • Exit analysis by reason: resignation, retirement, termination, transfer
  • Attrition rate by department - compared against benchmark
  • Monthly payroll cost by department: total and per-employee

Frequently asked questions

What HR analytics does ZenoHosp People provide?

ZenoHosp People provides live headcount by department and role, attendance rate trends, chronic absenteeism analysis, attrition tracking by reason, monthly payroll cost by department, and year-on-year comparisons.

How is attrition tracked?

Every employee exit is recorded with a reason code - resignation, retirement, termination, or transfer. Attrition rate is calculated per department per period and compared against configurable benchmarks.

Can department heads see their own HR analytics?

Yes. Department heads see their department's headcount, attendance rate, and payroll cost. HR and management see consolidated data across all departments.
