Leave Management

Staff apply for leave. Supervisor approves. Payroll updated. No forms.

Staff submit leave requests from the self-service portal. The supervisor receives a WhatsApp notification and approves or rejects. Approved leaves are reflected in the roster and attendance register. Leave balances update automatically. At month-end, approved leaves are applied to payroll.

  • Leave application from mobile self-service portal
  • Supervisor approval/rejection via WhatsApp notification
  • Approved leaves reflected in duty roster automatically
  • Leave types: CL, SL, EL, comp-off, LWP — all configured per policy
  • Leave balance updated immediately on approval

LOP & Attendance for Payroll

Every absent day accounted for. Payroll deductions calculated automatically.

When an employee is absent without an approved leave, ZenoHosp marks the day as Loss of Pay (LOP). At month-end, LOP days are calculated and passed to the payroll module for salary deduction. No manual reconciliation between attendance and payroll.

  • LOP marked automatically for absent days without approved leave
  • Late arrivals tracked: grace period configurable per shift
  • Half-day attendance rules configurable per department
  • LOP days passed to payroll for automatic deduction

Frequently asked questions

Which biometric devices does ZenoHosp support?

ZenoHosp supports most major biometric and RFID attendance devices via API integration or file-based import. Fingerprint, iris recognition, face recognition, and RFID card readers are all supported. Contact our implementation team for specific device compatibility.

How does the leave approval workflow work?

Staff submit a leave request from the self-service portal or mobile app. The supervisor receives a WhatsApp notification with the option to approve or reject. The employee is notified of the decision, and the leave balance and roster are updated automatically.

How are LOP deductions calculated?

When an employee is absent on a working day without an approved leave, ZenoHosp marks the day as Loss of Pay. At month-end, the LOP days are passed to the payroll module for salary deduction — no manual calculation.
