Frequently asked questions about hospital HR & payroll software in India

Does ZenoHosp People handle PF, ESI, and PT automatically?

Yes. Once salary structures are configured, ZenoHosp People calculates PF (12% employee + 13% employer), ESI (0.75% employee + 3.25% employer), and Professional Tax based on your state slab — every month, without manual input. Challans are generated ready for filing.

Can it integrate with our existing biometric device?

ZenoHosp People supports most major biometric and RFID attendance devices via API or file-based import. Our implementation team will handle the one-time integration during onboarding. Mobile punch-in with location capture is also available for staff on the go.

How does the leave approval process work?

An employee applies for leave through the self-service portal or mobile app. The request is routed to their immediate supervisor, who can approve or reject with a reason. Approved leave automatically updates the attendance register and payroll for the month.

Can I manage different shift patterns for different departments?

Yes. Each department can have its own set of shifts — morning, afternoon, evening, and night. Doctors, nurses, lab technicians, and admin staff can have entirely different shift structures managed from one roster screen.

Is Form 16 generated automatically?

Yes. At the end of the financial year, ZenoHosp People consolidates each employee's TDS, deductions, and perquisites and generates Form 16 Part A and Part B, ready for digital distribution to employees.

Does it integrate with ZenoHosp Finance for payroll journal entries?

Yes. When payroll is approved and disbursed, ZenoHosp People auto-posts the journal entries to the Finance module — salary payable, PF payable, ESI payable — keeping your books in sync without duplicate data entry.
