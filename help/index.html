Popular Questions

How do I reset a user's password?

Only a Company Admin can reset passwords. Navigate to People > HR Settings > Employee List, select the employee profile, and click "Reset Credentials".

How do I configure GST rates for new services?

Go to Finance > Configuration > Taxes. You can add new GST slabs and map them to specific service categories used in the HMS billing module.

Why isn't an OT Room showing up in the schedule?

Ensure the room hasn't been marked as "Under Maintenance" in the Asset module. Only operational rooms appear in the scheduling dropdown.
