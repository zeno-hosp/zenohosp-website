Navigating the DPDP Act & ABDM Compliance in 2026

Live Discussion • Thursday, July 16, 2026 • 4:00 PM IST • 60 mins

Webinar Description

Under the national Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM), the Indian government is rapidly standardizing digital records. Concurrently, the enforcement of India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act imposes strict legal frameworks around how patient health information is recorded, stored, and shared online.

This live executive discussion is designed for hospital founders, medical directors, and IT heads. Our speakers will walk through a step-by-step roadmap to implement secure, compliant electronic medical records (EMR), manage dynamic patient consent flows, and pass national privacy audits without disrupting day-to-day clinic operations.

Key Discussion Points

  • ABHA ID Creation: Best practices for front-office staffs to create and verify 14-digit ABHA numbers using Aadhaar OTP at patient registration.
  • Patient Consent Management: Setting up digital consent modules under the DPDP Act guidelines to log when, how, and why a patient authorized clinical history sharing.
  • HIP & HIU Compliance: Understanding the technical nodes required to link local EMR history securely to the national health grid.
  • Audit Readiness: Establishing automatic user timestamps and immutable logs to prepare your HMS for government verification.
