Every doctor knows this frustration.

You became a doctor to diagnose and treat patients — not to fight software, chase paper slips, or re-enter data that was already captured somewhere else.

01

A patient walks in and you know nothing about them

The previous visit notes are in a different system. Lab results are in the lab's folder. Prescriptions are on paper. You spend the first 3 minutes of every consultation asking the patient to repeat their history. For a returning patient, this should take 10 seconds.

02

You write a prescription — then nothing happens for 10 minutes

The paper slip goes to the front desk, gets photocopied, sent to the pharmacy, typed in again. By the time the patient reaches the counter, the pharmacist is hunting for what you wrote. ZenoHosp sends your prescription to the pharmacy queue the moment you save it. 3 seconds.

03

You order a lab test and then have to call to find out if it's done

The test gets ordered on paper, run in the lab, results entered in a separate system, printed, and then someone has to physically walk or call to tell you it's ready. With ZenoHosp, results appear in the patient's record the moment the lab technician validates them.

04

The software crashes during OPD rush — every time

200 patients. 11 AM. The system slows to a crawl. ZenoHosp was stress-tested at 300+ registrations per hour. And it works offline — if your internet drops, consultations continue and sync when connectivity returns.

05

You prescribe a drug and nobody flags the allergy

The patient's allergy is recorded somewhere — but not in front of you at the moment you're prescribing. ZenoHosp fires drug allergy alerts before the prescription is saved, cross-checking against the patient's known allergies and drug interaction database.

What ZenoHosp gives you in the consultation room

Every feature listed here came from watching Indian doctors work — specifically what slows them down and what they wish their system would just do.

Full patient history before they sit down

Every previous visit, diagnosis, prescription, lab result, and allergy — loaded the moment their OPD number is called. You start the consultation knowing them, not asking who they are.

Returning patient context in under 5 seconds

E-prescriptions that reach pharmacy in 3 seconds

Type or select the drug, dose, duration. Save. It's in the pharmacy queue. No paper. No phone call. No 10-minute gap while a slip gets photocopied and walked across the hospital.

Average: 3 seconds from save to pharmacy queue

Lab results on the same screen as the consultation

You order a test from the consultation screen. When the lab validates the result, it appears in the same patient record — no separate login, no phone call, no printed report to hunt for.

Results appear the moment the lab validates them

Drug allergy alerts before you save

If you prescribe something a patient has an allergy to — or two drugs with a known interaction — the system flags it before the prescription is saved. Not as a pop-up you click through. As a blocker that requires confirmation.

Cross-checks against patient's recorded allergies

Referrals in 3 clicks

Select the specialist, attach the relevant labs and clinical notes, send. The referral reaches the specialist's queue in under 10 seconds — with your notes and the patient's full history attached. No phone call. No paper slip the patient might lose.

Specialist sees your notes and labs immediately

Works on any device, even offline

Consultation notes, prescriptions, lab orders — everything works on a laptop, tablet, or desktop browser. If internet drops during OPD rush, ZenoHosp continues working and syncs automatically when connectivity returns.

Works offline, syncs when connectivity returns
