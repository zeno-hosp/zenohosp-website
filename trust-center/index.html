ZenoHosp Security

Your patient data.
Secured by design.

We built ZenoHosp to meet the highest international standards for privacy and security. From AES-256 encryption to strict DPDP compliance, your data is locked down, backed up, and strictly yours.

ISO 27001 Certified

Our information security management system is independently audited and certified to meet the ISO 27001 standard.

HIPAA Compliant

We adhere to strict HIPAA guidelines for safeguarding Electronic Protected Health Information (ePHI).

DPDP Act Ready

Fully compliant with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act. Patients maintain absolute rights over their data.

ABDM Integrated

Native integration with the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM) for seamless health record generation (ABHA).

Enterprise-Grade Infrastructure

AES-256 Bit Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256 bit encryption. We manage encryption keys using enterprise-grade Key Management Systems (KMS) so that even our own engineers cannot access raw patient data.

Role-Based Access (RBAC)

Granular access controls ensure staff only see what they need to. A nurse cannot see financial records, and billing staff cannot see clinical notes.

Immutable Audit Logs

Every single read, write, or delete action is permanently logged. You know exactly who accessed which patient record, at what time, from which IP address.

100% Data Residency in India

Your hospital's data never leaves the country. We host everything securely on AWS and Azure data centres located exclusively in Mumbai and Chennai, ensuring total compliance with local data localization mandates.
