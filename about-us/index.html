From a startup in Chennai to 500+ hospitals

The Frustration That Started It All

Our founders, while consulting at a 300-bed hospital in Chennai, discovered that nurses spent 40% of their shift on paperwork — and the existing HMS was the cause, not the cure. The idea for ZenoHosp was born.

2019

Incorporated as Zenlocare Pvt. Ltd.

The company was officially incorporated in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. We closed our first seed funding round and assembled a core team of 6 engineers and 2 clinical workflow consultants.

2020

First Live Hospital — ZenoHosp v1.0

We went live at HealthFirst Polyclinic, Coimbatore. The 12-doctor clinic saw a 75% reduction in patient wait times in the first month. The founding team celebrated with filter coffee and stayed up all night watching the dashboards.

2021

Full Suite Launch — 8 Modules

We shipped HMS, Inventory, Asset, OT Room, Finance, Pharmacy, Lab, and People — the complete ZenoHosp ecosystem. Reached 50 hospitals across Tamil Nadu and Karnataka. Crossed ₹1 Cr ARR.

2022

HIPAA Certification & Series A

ZenoHosp received formal HIPAA compliance certification. Closed our Series A funding round, enabling us to expand our implementation team and open a dedicated customer success office in Bengaluru.

2023

500+ Hospitals. 12 Million Patients.

Today, ZenoHosp serves 500+ healthcare facilities, has processed over 12 million patient encounters, and maintains a 98% customer retention rate — the highest in the Indian HMS market. We're just getting started.

2024–25

What we believe

We build for clinicians, not administrators.

Every feature gets tested by watching real nurses, doctors, and front-desk staff work. If it adds a click without adding value, it gets cut. The doctor's time at the bedside is more important than our product roadmap.

Patient data is not our product.

AES-256 encryption, zero-trust access, full audit logging — on every deployment, not just enterprise plans. Your patient records belong to your hospital, always. We provide complete data exports. We don't lock you in. We've never sold or shared a byte of patient data.

We go live with you — not just for you.

Every hospital gets a dedicated implementation partner. When something breaks at 2am on a Monday, a real person picks up the phone — not a ticket system. We don't disappear after go-live. 98% of our customers renew. That's not marketing; that's why we sleep at night.
