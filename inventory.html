Frequently Asked Questions about Hospital Inventory Software in India

What is hospital inventory management software?

Hospital inventory management software is a system that helps healthcare facilities track medical supplies, manage stock levels, automate reordering, and monitor expiry dates. ZenoHosp offers a comprehensive solution for Indian hospitals.

Does it support barcode scanning?

Yes, ZenoHosp Inventory module supports barcode scanning for quick stock entry, verification, and tracking. This speeds up inventory audits and reduces manual errors.

Can it automatically reorder supplies?

Yes, the system can be configured to automatically generate purchase orders when stock falls below minimum levels, ensuring critical supplies are never out of stock.

Does it integrate with other hospital departments?

Yes, ZenoHosp Inventory integrates with HMS, OT, and Pharmacy modules, automatically updating stock levels when supplies are consumed by different departments.
